Have your say on capital budget requests for the Brooklyn CB6 district!

Each year Community Boards establish and submit a prioritized list of budget requests for the Mayor, City Council and Borough President to consider when determining the City budget.

Give input today and help shape those requests.

The data from this project will be used to identify and justify sought-after Capital budget projects. Public input will help inform Community Board submissions.
