Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long the map open for?

The map will be open for contribution until 30 September 2014.

2. What happens to the data collected?

The results and data of this project will:

  • Be used by the Property and Campus Services Department to best allocate future bike parking facilities.
  • Be used as part of a student's thesis research.
  • Be spatially visualised and used as feedback for those who provided their email.

3. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (spots may be edited or omitted).

4. What is I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email Anthony (anthony@crowdspot.com.au) and he'll help sort it out.

Yes, at this stage we're trialling the map at only the Parkville Campus. We hope there will be opportunities at other UniMelb Campuses in the future.
